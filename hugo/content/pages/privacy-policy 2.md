## Overview

This website, The Sovereign Miner (sovereign-miner.com), is operated by Cooper Oscarfono as a personal blog. This policy explains what information is collected when you visit this site, how it is used, and your rights in relation to that information.

This site operates in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

## Information Collected

### Information you provide directly

If you use the contact form on this site, your name, email address, and message content are collected for the purpose of responding to your enquiry. This information is not used for any other purpose and is not shared with third parties.

### Information collected automatically

When you visit this site, standard web server logs and analytics tools may collect information including your IP address, browser type, referring pages, and pages visited. This information is used in aggregate to understand how the site is used and to improve its content. It is not used to identify individual visitors.

### Cookies

This site uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. A cookie notice is displayed on your first visit and you may accept or decline. If you decline, no non-essential cookies will be set. You can also control cookies through your browser settings at any time.

## Third Party Services

This site uses the following third party services which may collect data independently under their own privacy policies:

- **Ko-fi** — if you choose to make a donation via the Buy Me a Beer link, your transaction is processed by Ko-fi. Their privacy policy is available at ko-fi.com.
- **Google Analytics** — used to understand site traffic in aggregate. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com.
- **Mailchimp** — if you subscribe to the mailing list, your email address and name are stored and processed by Mailchimp (Intuit Inc.). You may unsubscribe at any time using the link included in every email. Their privacy policy is available at mailchimp.com.

## Data Storage and Security

This site does not operate a database of user information. Contact form submissions are processed via Formspree and subject to their data handling practices. No personal information collected through this site is sold or shared with advertisers.

## Links to Other Sites

This site contains links to external websites. This privacy policy applies only to sovereign-miner.com. You should review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

## Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles you have the right to request access to any personal information held about you, and to request correction of that information if it is inaccurate. To make such a request, contact holla@sovereign-miner.com.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The date of the most recent revision is shown at the top of this page.
